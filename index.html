PICK N' MATCH

Pick N Match is a classic memory matching game designed to test and improve your memory skills. The objective of the game is to reveal and match pairs of hidden images without the pressure of a time limit. Players must use their powers of observation and recollection to find and match pairs, As you progress through the game, you can turn up the difficulty, with more cards and a wider range of images to match. Be careful though, as each incorrect guess will cost you one of your precious lives. If you lose all lives, the game ends, making Pick N Match a challenging and addictive game suitable for players of all ages and experience levels.
